Biography

I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (minor in Economics) from National Taiwan University in 2012. I joined the Ph.D. program at Cornell as a Jacobs Fellow in Fall 2013. I am a member of the Networks Group led by Dr. Kevin Tang. My research interests include network optimization and algorithms.
